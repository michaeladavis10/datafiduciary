The Problem with Traditional Models

Finding the right partner to grow your business is a critical decision, but the landscape can be frustrating. You're often faced with a few familiar, and flawed, paths:

The Enterprise Strategists (McKinsey, Deloitte, BCG): Their models are built for Fortune 500 budgets. This can lead to beautiful presentations from bright junior teams, but may not offer the hands-on, senior-level partnership you need.

The Business Coaches: They offer valuable mindset and accountability coaching, but often lack the technical, hands-on expertise to actually build and implement the data-driven solutions your business needs to scale.

The Software Vendors: They promise a single piece of software will solve your problems, forcing your unique business processes into their rigid system. Then, to get the full value from your investment, you discover the necessity of hiring expensive implementation partners to make it all work.

The Generic Accelerators: This world is a minefield. A quick search on Reddit reveals a crazy mix of reviews, with many programs being called outright scams (see for yourself: Reddit Reviews).

The DataBuddy Solution

We don't just deliver ideas, and we don't sell software. We are a true growth partner who builds the solution and shares in the outcome. Our model is designed to be completely different and is founded on mutual trust.

We start with a low upfront cost, and you choose the amount. It’s not about our bottom line; it’s a shared investment to ensure we are both fully committed to seeing the project through to success.

From there, the rest is on us. The bulk of our payment only comes from a profit-sharing agreement after we deliver. We put in the work, but if you don't get results, you don't pay anything else.

It’s that simple. We succeed only when you do.
