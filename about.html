A Different Breed of Partner

DataBuddy was founded on a simple premise: business owners deserve a technical partner who operates with an owner's mindset. Our mission is to transform your data from a source of complexity into your most powerful tool for driving profit and strategic growth.

A Perspective Forged in Three Worlds

We combine three crucial experiences to be an advocate for owners, not just a vendor.

Enterprise-Grade Data Systems

After years building the data engines for giants like LinkedIn, TripAdvisor, and Snowflake, a simple truth became clear: most businesses are sold complex, expensive solutions they don't need. We apply the principles of world-class data strategy, but tailored for the practical, bottom-line realities of running an SMB.

Hands-On Business Ownership

We've been in your seat—making payroll, managing cash flow, and feeling the weight of every decision. As active business owners who have built, acquired, and operated multiple SMBs, we don't just understand your challenges; we've lived them. This shared reality is why we treat your business with the same rigor and respect we give our own.

Academic Rigor & Practical Education

True partnership means empowerment. With experience teaching data analytics to business students at universities like Northeastern and Santa Clara, we excel at making complex topics simple. We don't just deliver a solution; we ensure your team understands the 'why' behind it, building lasting data literacy and independence within your organization.

The Quiet Professional

We believe substance wins. In a world saturated with hype and buzzwords, our focus is on deep work and tangible outcomes. Our approach is defined by listening more than we talk, analyzing before we advise, and delivering results instead of just presentations.

We don't need to be the loudest voice in the room because our work speaks for itself. This ethos—the Quiet Professional—is a commitment to rigor, clarity, and a relentless focus on what truly moves the needle for your business.

Our Enterprise-Grade Foundation

Our approach is forged by solving high-stakes problems at the world's most iconic tech companies. This isn't just our resume—it's the enterprise-grade foundation of expertise we bring to your business.

The Challenge: To fuel a historic IPO, Snowflake's sales team needed a single source of truth for their CRM and predictive insights to operate at peak efficiency.

The Work: This role involved building the core GTM data infrastructure using cutting-edge tools and developing ML models for sales forecasting.

The Result: These foundational systems were critical in supporting the sales engine that powered Snowflake to one of the largest software IPOs in history.

The Challenge: LinkedIn's ads division was experiencing explosive growth, which required data systems that were not only scalable but also deeply integrated with operational procedures.

The Work: This role focused on building robust revenue, ads, and forecasting tools, architecting them to be resilient by integrating deeply with team workflows.

The Result: These systems directly powered the ads team's growth, proved durable enough to support the company through its acquisition, and are still in use over 10 years later.

The Challenge: The FlipKey division needed to accelerate growth to become the core of TripAdvisor's vacation rental strategy, requiring a platform for rapid, data-driven experimentation.

The Work: Key contributions included building the A/B testing framework and a behavioral engine for search to remove friction from the user signup process.

The Result: The successful growth engine built at FlipKey became the foundation that would ultimately power TripAdvisor's entire vacation rental platform.
